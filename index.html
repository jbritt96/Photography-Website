About me

I'm Erin Ehlers and I'm a twenty-one year old photographer based in Saint George, Utah. I fell in love with photography soon after I bought my first camera in high school, and have been taking pictures ever since. After taking several photography classes in both high school and college, and becoming certified in digital photography, I continued to pursue my interest by photographing a wide range of clients and traveling around beautiful locations in the western States for wildlife and landscape shoots. My professional experience includes shooting bridal sessions, senior portraits, missionary photography, and family portraits. I'd love to help capture your next important moment, so contact me to discuss making your vision a reality!
